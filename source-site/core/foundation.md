## Our Mission

Holistic Healthcare for All

We believe in transforming the future of healthcare by embedding integrative medicine and technology into hospitals, schools, and homes. Our vision is to make whole-person care accessible to all—regardless of income—through education, advocacy, clinical collaboration, and digital innovation. By uniting holistic principles with modern tools, we aim to empower individuals, improve outcomes, and create sustainable wellness across communities.

Dr. Knights Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit advancing health equity through community innovation, education, and advocacy.

We support underserved residents across Washington, D.C. and Maryland by delivering culturally grounded wellness education, mental health workshops, and chronic disease navigation programs. Our work focuses on dismantling systemic barriers by integrating clinical expertise, lived experience, and digital innovation to improve access and outcomes in communities disproportionately impacted by illness.

Through strategic partnerships with local organizations, technologists, and residents, we co-create scalable solutions that promote mental wellness, reduce preventable disease, and transform how whole-person care is delivered in historically excluded communities.

## How We Take Action

Funding Holistic Treatments for Patients in Need

We provide direct financial assistance to individuals who cannot afford holistic healthcare. From cancer patients seeking complementary treatments to those battling chronic illnesses, our grants help cover the cost of naturopathic consultations, herbal medicine, and other integrative therapies that support healing and recovery.
