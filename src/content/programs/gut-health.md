At Nirvana Holistic Medicine, we are committed to helping you feel your best from the inside out. The Gut Health Program supports digestive health, gentle detoxification, and sustainable weight management through thoughtfully designed wellness offerings focused on whole-food nutrition, targeted supplementation, and supportive in-clinic therapies.

## What This Program Addresses

Many of our patients arrive with symptoms that trace back to the gut: bloating, irregular digestion, food sensitivities, persistent fatigue, skin concerns, and difficulty maintaining a healthy weight. Working from the assumption that gut health drives systemic resilience, we use a combination of nutrition, supplementation, detoxification, and gentle in-clinic therapies to help you restore balance.

## What's Included

- A thorough wellness consultation including history, current symptoms, and food patterns
- Personalized nutrition guidance focused on whole foods and digestive support
- Medical-grade supplementation tailored to your situation
- Optional in-clinic services such as colon hydrotherapy and infrared sauna to support gentle detoxification
- Ongoing follow-up to track progress and adjust the plan

## What to Expect

Your initial consultation is a long, unhurried conversation. We map out the symptoms you have noticed, the food patterns and stressors that may be contributing, and the supports we want to put in place. From there, we build a plan that fits your life and revisit it at regular intervals.
