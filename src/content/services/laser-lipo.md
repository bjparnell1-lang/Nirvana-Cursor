## What Is Laser Lipo?

Low-level laser therapy is a non-invasive technology used to support body contouring as part of a broader approach to wellness. It is most effective when paired with personalized nutrition guidance, movement work, and consistent follow-up.

## Benefits People Often Notice

- Targeted body contouring support
- A complement to nutrition and lifestyle changes already underway
- A non-invasive option for those exploring alternatives to more aggressive procedures

## What to Expect During Your Session

A practitioner walks you through the session, positions the laser pads on the target area, and stays nearby for the duration of the treatment. Sessions are quiet and require no anesthesia. You leave the clinic able to return to normal activity.

## Who This Is Good For

Patients pursuing measurable body composition changes alongside our Diabetes Reversal or Hormone Harmony programs, or anyone interested in non-invasive body contouring as part of a broader wellness practice.
