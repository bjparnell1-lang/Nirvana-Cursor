### The Diabetes Program at Nirvana Holistic Medicine

A Holistic Approach to Reversing Type 2 Diabetes and Restoring Lasting Health

At Nirvana Holistic Medicine, our Diabetes Program is designed to support patients in achieving optimal health and, for many with Type 2 diabetes, reversing their condition. By combining advanced medical and holistic therapies, we empower patients with the tools for sustainable lifestyle change that promote blood sugar control, address underlying hormone imbalances, and target food addiction patterns.

#### Tailored Care for Diabetes Management

- Comprehensive Support for Type 1 and Type 2 Diabetes:


Our program provides personalized care aimed at improving overall health and blood sugar control. For individuals with Type 1 diabetes, we focus on preventing organ damage and achieving optimal blood sugar control. For Type 2 diabetes, our primary goal is to reverse diabetes by targeting the root causes of blood sugar imbalance. We address hormone imbalances, improve insulin sensitivity, and help patients identify and overcome food addiction patterns. Short-term GLP-1 receptor agonists are available as needed, but our focus is on equipping patients with sustainable tools for lasting change.

- Customized Nutrition and Supplementation:


Each patient receives a customized nutrition plan tailored to their individual health needs, preferences, and goals. Our program includes medical-grade supplements and herbs designed to enhance metabolic health and ensure safety in conjunction with any existing medications. Additionally, we utilize advanced testing to identify hormone imbalances, inflammation, insulin sensitivity, and gut health, guiding personalized care and improving outcomes.


#### Program Highlights

- Virtual Coaching: Our program is primarily conducted virtually, allowing patients to participate from the comfort of their homes. This includes weekly group coaching for support and one-on-one coaching for personalized goal-setting and guidance, ensuring that care is both accessible and individualized.

- IV Therapy for Advanced Cases: For patients with advanced Type 1 or Type 2 diabetes who require more intensive support, IV therapy is available as a key component of their treatment plan. This is offered in a clinical setting to ensure the highest level of care.
