## Empowering Patients to Manage Diabetes Effectively

The Diabetes Program at Nirvana Holistic Medicine is designed to equip patients with the knowledge and tools to manage diabetes effectively. Our program combines conventional medical approaches with holistic strategies, with attention to:

- **Lifestyle Changes:** personalized exercise and stress management tailored to each patient's needs.
- **Nutritional Support:** balanced, whole-food approaches that stabilize blood sugar and support overall well-being.
- **Integrative Therapies:** acupuncture and herbal support to enhance metabolic function and quality of life.

Through education, support, and continuous monitoring, the program addresses physical, emotional, and lifestyle factors of health.

## The Nirvana Protocol — Comprehensive, Root-Cause-Oriented Care

A holistic approach to reversing Type 2 diabetes and supporting lasting health.

At Nirvana Holistic Medicine, our Diabetes Program supports patients in achieving optimal health and, for many with Type 2 diabetes, working toward reversal. By combining advanced medical and holistic therapies, we equip patients with sustainable lifestyle tools that support blood sugar control, address underlying hormone imbalances, and target food-pattern challenges.

### Tailored Care for Diabetes Management

**Comprehensive Support for Type 1 and Type 2 Diabetes**

For Type 1 diabetes, we focus on preventing organ damage and optimizing blood sugar control. For Type 2 diabetes, our primary goal is to support reversal by addressing the upstream drivers of blood sugar imbalance — hormone imbalance, insulin sensitivity, and food patterns. Short-term GLP-1 receptor agonists are available as needed, but our focus is on equipping patients with sustainable tools for lasting change.

**Customized Nutrition and Supplementation**

Each patient receives a customized nutrition plan tailored to their needs, preferences, and goals. The program includes medical-grade supplements and herbs designed to support metabolic health alongside any existing medications. Advanced testing identifies hormone imbalances, inflammation, insulin sensitivity, and gut health to guide personalized care.

### Program Highlights

- **Virtual Coaching:** primarily virtual delivery, with weekly group coaching and one-on-one sessions for accessibility and individualized care.
- **IV Therapy for Advanced Cases:** for patients requiring more intensive support, IV therapy is available in a clinical setting.
