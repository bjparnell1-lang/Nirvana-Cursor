High-dose ozone therapy delivers concentrated medical-grade ozone gas via the bloodstream. The protocol is reserved for situations that warrant intensive intervention — chronic infections such as Lyme disease, resistant bacterial or viral conditions, autoimmune dysregulation, and chronic pain or fatigue.

Patients pursuing high-dose ozone are medically cleared in advance, and the protocol is administered in a fully clinical setting with appropriate monitoring. This is one of our most specialized infusions and is used as part of a broader integrative plan.
