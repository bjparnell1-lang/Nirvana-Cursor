Mistletoe extract — known by the brand name Iscador in many integrative oncology settings — is delivered intravenously to ensure optimal absorption and precise, individualized dosing. The protocol has been used for decades in European integrative oncology and is increasingly part of the integrative approach for cancer patients in the U.S.

Patients frequently choose mistletoe as part of an integrative approach for supporting immune response, quality of life, and overall resilience during active oncology care. As with all of our specialty IVs, mistletoe is administered after medical clearance and in close coordination with your oncology team.
