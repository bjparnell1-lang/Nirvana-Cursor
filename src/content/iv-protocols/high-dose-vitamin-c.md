High-dose Vitamin C, infused at concentrations between 50 and 100 grams, delivers ascorbate directly into the bloodstream — bypassing the gut barriers that limit oral absorption and reaching plasma levels that simply are not achievable by mouth.

This protocol is most often used as part of an integrative approach for patients navigating cancer care, severe or persistent infections, or significant immune challenges. Many patients also report improved energy and a sense of accelerated recovery in the days following an infusion.

We medically clear every patient before high-dose Vitamin C therapy. For patients in active oncology care, our protocol explicitly supports — and never replaces — your conventional treatment.
