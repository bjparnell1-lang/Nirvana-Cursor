Alpha-Lipoic Acid (ALA) is a versatile antioxidant that helps neutralize free radicals and reduce oxidative stress. Patients frequently choose IV ALA for its breadth of supportive effects — protecting nerve cells, supporting liver function and detoxification, and contributing to metabolic and cardiovascular health.

This infusion is commonly used as part of an integrative approach for patients navigating diabetic neuropathy, multiple sclerosis, and conditions where oxidative stress is part of the picture.
