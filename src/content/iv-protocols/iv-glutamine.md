Glutamine is the most abundant amino acid in the body — and it's especially important for the cells of the gut lining and the immune system. IV glutamine delivers a concentrated dose directly to the tissues that need it most.

Patients commonly pursue this infusion when navigating gastrointestinal challenges (compromised gut lining, inflammatory bowel disease, post-surgical recovery), or to support muscle recovery during intense rehabilitation or training.
