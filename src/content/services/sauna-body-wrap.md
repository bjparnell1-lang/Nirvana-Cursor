## A Quiet Ritual of Warmth and Recovery

Step away from the daily grind and give your body the care it needs. Our sauna and body wrap services are designed to help you unwind, reset, and restore balance.

A 50-minute full-body massage can be paired with a 30-minute infrared sauna session, where gentle heat works to support circulation and the body's natural processes of elimination, leaving you feeling lighter and refreshed.

## Benefits People Often Notice

- Sense of deep relaxation and stress relief
- Support for circulation
- Support for the body's natural detoxification pathways
- A sense of warmth and ease in the muscles

## What to Expect

You arrive a few minutes early for an unhurried check-in. We bring you to a private treatment space, walk you through the session, and leave you to settle in. Afterward, you take time to rehydrate and rest before heading back into your day.

## Who This Is Good For

Anyone navigating high stress, chronic muscle tension, or supporting a broader detoxification or recovery practice. Often paired with our gut, autoimmune, and hormone programs.
