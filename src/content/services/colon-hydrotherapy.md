## What Is Colon Hydrotherapy?

Colon hydrotherapy — sometimes called colonic irrigation — is a gentle infusion of warm, filtered water into the colon to support the body's natural elimination of waste. It is performed by a certified therapist using FDA-registered, closed-system equipment in a private, comfortable setting.

Colonics are not a treatment for any specific disease, but for many people they support the body's ability to feel resilient by gently relieving the burden of accumulated waste — what some practitioners describe as auto-intoxication.

## Benefits People Often Notice

- Relief from constipation, bloating, and abdominal heaviness
- Increased energy and mental clarity
- Support for nutrient absorption
- Complement to weight management and detoxification programs
- Support for the gut microbiome

## What to Expect During Your Session

- A private, quiet treatment room
- A licensed professional guiding you through the entire session
- FDA-registered, closed-system equipment in a discreet, odor-free process
- Sessions typically lasting 45–60 minutes
- Post-session aftercare guidance to support how you feel afterward
