# Nirvana Holistic Medicine - Project & Content Plan

## Project Goal
Build a premium, luxury holistic medical marketing website for Nirvana Holistic Medicine. The site should feel custom, high-end, clinical, warm, trustworthy, and patient-facing.

**Core Philosophy:**
- Root-focused language(but use varied language: underlying drivers, deeper patterns, whole-person assessment, restoring balance).
- Whole-person and integrative.
- Supportive for chronic conditions, cancer care, diabetes, hormones, autoimmune concerns, detox, and wellness.
- Calm, credible, and medically thoughtful.
- Conversion-focused, with clear appointment CTAs.

## Site Structure & Navigation
Top navigation: Home, About Us, Medical Programs with drop down selection of programs, Services with drop down selection of services, Dr Knights Foundation, Book Appointment.

## Design & Layout Direction
- **Vibe:** Natural greens, tans, browns, warm neutrals, and restrained gold accents.
- **Imagery:** Premium realistic photography. Selective use of people. Human/emotional imagery for medical programs. 
- **Logo:** Make sure the logo is placed seamlessly on its background with no flaws.  Files: `public/images/brand/nirvana-logo-transparent.png`.
