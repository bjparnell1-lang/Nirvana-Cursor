## What Is a Foot Detox?

A foot detox uses an ionic foot bath to support the body's natural pathways of elimination. It is best understood as one quiet element within a broader detoxification practice, often combined with nutrition, movement, and other in-clinic supports.

## Benefits People Often Notice

- A sense of calm and reduced stress
- Support for circulation
- A gentle complement to a broader detoxification practice
- Sometimes: relief from puffiness or heaviness in the feet and lower legs

## What to Expect

You arrive a few minutes early. Your feet are placed in a warm bath of mineral-rich water, and the session lasts about 30 minutes. The experience is quiet — most patients use the time to rest, read, or simply close their eyes.

## Who This Is Good For

Patients participating in our Gut Health or Autoimmune Support programs, or anyone exploring gentle, supportive detoxification practices.
