## What Is Fit3D Body Scanning?

The Fit3D scanner is a body-composition tool that takes precise measurements without the hassle of tape measures or guesswork. The scan creates a baseline you can revisit at consistent intervals to track changes in posture, body composition, and circumference.

## Benefits

- Precise, repeatable body composition measurements
- A baseline to track progress over weeks, months, and years
- Visual data that supports motivation and accountability
- Useful for personalizing fitness, nutrition, and metabolic health plans

Body composition is just one aspect of overall health. We always interpret it alongside other markers — blood pressure, cholesterol, lab work, and how you feel — during your consultation.

## What to Expect

You step onto the scanner platform, follow simple positioning instructions, and the scan completes in about a minute. We discuss the results during your wellness consultation, where they inform the rest of your care plan.

## Who This Is Good For

Patients in our Diabetes Reversal, Hormone Harmony, or general wellness programs who want measurable, repeatable body composition data, and anyone tracking long-term changes alongside an existing fitness or nutrition practice.
