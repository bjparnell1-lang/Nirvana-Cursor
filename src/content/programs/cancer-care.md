## Who We Serve

### Integrative Route

Our Cancer Care Support Program is designed for those undergoing chemotherapy, radiation, or post-surgery recovery. We provide integrative therapies — IV nutrient therapy, acupuncture, nutritional guidance, detox support, and stress management — to enhance recovery, reduce side effects, and improve quality of life. Working closely with your oncologist and care team, we create a tailored plan that supports every aspect of your healing journey.

### Hospice Designated Patients

For those seeking comfort and an improved quality of life, our holistic therapies provide essential support that enhances resilience and alleviates symptoms. We understand that the journey is far from over for those who have been referred to hospice, and we are dedicated to offering individualized support that addresses both physical and emotional needs.

### Holistic Route

For those prioritizing natural medicine, our integrative options encompass therapies designed to strengthen the immune system, support detoxification, and provide effective symptom relief. These may include herbal supplements, nutritional counseling, acupuncture, and lifestyle modifications tailored to your individual health needs. While we do not recommend relying solely on natural medicine, we are here to support you throughout your journey, offering guidance and resources that complement your overall treatment plan.

## The Nirvana Protocol

Our cancer care protocol is individualized and designed to address underlying health drivers and support recovery:

- In-Depth Wellness Assessments: complete health evaluations for a customized care plan.
- Advanced Medical and Genetic Testing: precision tests to guide targeted therapies.
- Detox Programs: IV detox, herbal detox, and water fasting options to prepare the body.
- Nutrition: individualized meal plans to bolster immunity and address malnutrition.
- Exercise Programs: customized physical activities to maintain strength.
- Educational Support and Mentorship: classes and holistic coaching to support your journey.

### Targeted Integrative Therapies

Carefully selected to work in conjunction with cancer treatments, aiming to strengthen the immune system, reduce inflammation, and improve quality of life:

- High-Dose Vitamin C IV Therapy
- Alpha-Lipoic Acid (ALA) IV Therapy
- Nutrient IVs customized to support hydration, immune health, and recovery
- Mistletoe Injections — immune-modulating therapy with anti-inflammatory effects
- Ozone Therapy
- IV B Vitamins
- Colonics
- Acupuncture
- Herbal Supplement Plans
- Low-Dose Naltrexone

### Side Effect Support with Integrative Options

Our therapies target specific side effects to support comfort and resilience throughout treatment:

- Fatigue: high-dose Vitamin C, hyperbaric oxygen therapy, acupuncture
- Nausea: acupuncture, nutrient IVs, herbal supplements
- Neuropathy and nerve pain: IV B vitamins, acupuncture, low-dose naltrexone
- Weight changes and cachexia: nutritional support, amino acid IV therapy, exercise
- Digestive issues: colonics, herbal supplements, nutrition guidance
- Anxiety and stress: acupuncture, mistletoe therapy, low-dose naltrexone
