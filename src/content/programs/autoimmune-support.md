Living with an autoimmune condition often brings persistent symptoms — pain, fatigue, and inflammation — that can be challenging to manage. Our Comprehensive Autoimmune Program is designed to help you take control of your health through an integrative approach that combines advanced medical treatments, nutritional guidance, and natural therapies tailored to your individual needs.

## The Nirvana Protocol — Comprehensive, Root-Cause-Oriented Care

This program treats the whole person rather than only alleviating symptoms. It encompasses advanced medical interventions, nutritional support, and natural therapies tailored to your situation.

### What to Expect

1. **Personalized Assessment** — a thorough evaluation of your medical history, current symptoms, and lifestyle factors. We use this to create a customized treatment plan.
2. **Advanced Medical Treatments** — evidence-based interventions aimed at reducing inflammation and modulating the immune response, including targeted therapies and IV infusions.
3. **Nutritional Guidance** — individualized meal plans focused on anti-inflammatory foods, nutrient-density, and any specific dietary considerations you have.
4. **Natural Therapies** — herbal medicine, acupuncture, and mind-body techniques to support healing and balance.
5. **Ongoing Support and Monitoring** — regular follow-ups, treatment-plan adjustments, and education on self-management strategies.

## Program Options

We offer three flexible programs designed to meet your goals — short-term relief through long-term healing.

### 1. Six-Week Program — $750

- A two-week detox to jump-start your health
- A one-month customized program including:
  - Medical-grade supplements tailored to your specific autoimmune condition
  - Nutritional counseling and tracking
  - Virtual or in-person medical evaluation to monitor progress and adjust treatment
- IV therapy options: one session of Vitamin C (25–50g), ALA, low-dose ozone, glutathione, or mistletoe to provide immune and antioxidant support. Ozone and low-dose naltrexone are available to support anti-inflammatory effects.

This program is for those seeking a focused, intensive intervention to support symptom management and reduce inflammation.

### 2. Three-Month Program

For those who need sustained support to address more complex autoimmune challenges.

**Virtual: $275 per month** — monthly medical-grade supplements, nutritional counseling and tracking, remote medical evaluation and lab work.

**In-Person: $475 per month** — all virtual benefits, plus access to in-person IV therapies (Vitamin C, ALA, low-dose ozone, glutathione, mistletoe) including ozone and low-dose naltrexone.

Both options include weekly nutritionist consultations for the first month and biweekly afterward, plus a customized detox kit. Lab evaluations every six weeks.

### 3. Six-Month Program

For those managing chronic or advanced autoimmune conditions, the six-month program provides comprehensive, ongoing care.

**Virtual: $275 per month** — all services from the three-month virtual program, with monthly lab evaluations and customized adjustments.

**In-Person: $475 per month** — all virtual services, plus in-person IV therapies (Vitamin C, ALA, low-dose ozone, glutathione, mistletoe) including ozone and low-dose naltrexone, plus monthly detox treatments such as colonics or sauna sessions.

In both the three- and six-month programs, you meet with your nutritionist weekly during the first month, then biweekly. Lab evaluations occur every six weeks.

## Additional Services and Flexibility

Virtual participants receive labs and medical evaluations remotely, with the option to add mistletoe injections for home use at $475/month. In-person participants have access to colonics, sauna treatments, and a customizable IV therapy menu informed by lab results and ongoing medical evaluations.

## Why Nirvana Holistic Medicine

We focus on whole-body healing. Our integrative approach addresses the contributing factors of autoimmune conditions by blending medical science with natural, supportive therapies. Personalized care evolves alongside your body's changes.
