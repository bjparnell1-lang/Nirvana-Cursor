## A First, Unhurried Conversation

The wellness consultation is the starting point for every Nirvana medical program and for many of our standalone services. We block out a long, unhurried window so we can ask the right questions, listen carefully, and build a plan that fits your life.

## What to Expect

- A thorough review of your history — including current concerns, past medical history, family history, lifestyle, and what you have already tried
- A physical assessment as appropriate
- Lab work or advanced testing if it will help guide your care
- A clear, written summary of the path forward — including which Nirvana programs or services we recommend, the order they make sense in, and why

We treat this conversation as the foundation for everything that follows. The clearer the picture we build together, the better the care we can offer.

## Who This Is Good For

Anyone considering one of our medical programs, anyone navigating a complex or unclear health picture, and anyone who wants a thorough integrative perspective alongside care they may already be receiving elsewhere.

## How It Pairs With Our Programs

Every Nirvana medical program begins with a wellness consultation. Standalone consultations are also available — sometimes one conversation is exactly what's needed to know what to do next.
