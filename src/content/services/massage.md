## A Considered Approach to Bodywork

Our massage offerings range from deep, full-body relaxation work to targeted contouring sessions. Tell us what you are working with — chronic tension, recovery from a tough training cycle, post-treatment fatigue, or a focused goal — and we tailor the session accordingly.

## Contouring Massage

A specialty session combining manual therapy, compression techniques, and infrared heat. Used to support body contouring, this session can be a stand-alone experience or a complement to your current weight-management or detox program.

## Benefits People Often Notice

- Relief from chronic muscle tension
- Support for circulation and recovery
- A sense of calm and stress reduction
- Targeted contouring support when paired with broader nutrition and movement work

## What to Expect

A short check-in at the start of your session lets your therapist understand what you need that day. The treatment itself is quiet — your job is to rest. Afterward, plenty of water and a few minutes of rest before you head back into the day.

## Who This Is Good For

Patients in any of our medical programs who want a supportive, restorative bodywork practice, and anyone looking for a quiet hour of recovery in a calm clinical setting.
