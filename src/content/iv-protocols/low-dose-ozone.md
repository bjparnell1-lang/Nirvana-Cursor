Low-dose ozone therapy involves infusing medical-grade ozone gas into the bloodstream at carefully measured concentrations. The protocol supports immune function, oxygen utilization, and cellular health — and is frequently chosen by patients managing chronic fatigue, autoimmune dysregulation, arthritis, or cardiovascular concerns.

As part of an integrative approach, low-dose ozone is most often used alongside other supportive therapies — IV nutrients, lifestyle work, and targeted supplementation — to support overall resilience.
