### Integrative Route

Our Cancer Care Support Program is designed for those undergoing chemotherapy, radiation, or post-surgery recovery. We provide integrative therapies—IV nutrient therapy, acupuncture, nutritional guidance, detox support, and stress management—to enhance recovery, reduce side effects, and improve quality of life. Working closely with your oncologist and care team, we create a tailored plan that supports every aspect of your healing journey. Our goal is to offer the most effective and compassionate care possible, empowering you to achieve optimal health and well-being.

### Hospice Designated Patients

For those seeking comfort and an improved quality of life, our holistic therapies provide essential support that enhances resilience and alleviates symptoms. We understand that the journey is far from over for those who have been referred to hospice, and we are dedicated to offering individualized support that addresses both physical and emotional needs. Our compassionate approach ensures you feel empowered and cared for throughout this process.

​

### Holistic Route

For those prioritizing natural medicine, our integrative options encompass a range of therapies specifically designed to strengthen the immune system, detoxify the body, and provide effective symptom relief. These therapies may herbal supplements, nutritional counseling, acupuncture, and lifestyle modifications tailored to your individual health needs. While we do not recommend relying solely on natural medicine, we are here to support you throughout your journey, offering guidance and resources that complement your overall treatment plan. Our goal is to empower you with the tools and knowledge to enhance your health and well-being, ensuring a holistic approach to your care.

## The Nirvana Protocol:

### ​​​​​​Our cancer care protocol is individualized and designed to address underlying health issues and reduce recurrence:

- In-Depth Wellness Assessments: Complete health evaluations for a customized care plan.

- Advanced Medical and Genetic Testing: Precision tests to guide targeted therapies.

- Detox Programs: IV detox, herbal detox and water fasting options to prepare the body.

- Nutrition : Individualized meal plans to bolster immunity and address malnutrition.

- Exercise Programs: Customized physical activities to maintain strength.

- Educational Support and Mentorship: Classes and holistic coaching to aid your journey.


### Targeted Integrative Therapies

Our therapies are carefully selected to work in conjunction with cancer treatments, aiming to strengthen the immune system, reduce inflammation, and improve quality of life:

- High-Dose Vitamin C IV Therapy: Enhances immune function and supports cellular health.

- Alpha-Lipoic Acid (ALA) IV Therapy: Reduces oxidative stress and supports liver health.

- Nutrient IVs: Customized to support hydration, immune health, and recovery.

- Mistletoe Injections: Immune-boosting therapy with anti-inflammatory effects.

- Ozone Therapy: Enhances oxygenation and reduces inflammation.

- Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (Coming Soon): Promotes tissue healing and reduces inflammation.

- IV B Vitamins: Alleviates nerve pain and helps combat neuropathy.

- Colonics: Supports digestive health and detoxification.

- Acupuncture: Relieves pain, reduces stress, and alleviates nausea.

- Herbal Supplement Plans: Individualized for symptom management.

- Low-Dose Naltrexone: Immune modulation to support wellness.


### Side Effect Management with Integrative Options

Our therapies target specific side effects to support comfort and resilience throughout your treatment:

- Fatigue: High-dose Vitamin C, Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy, and Acupuncture.

- Nausea and Vomiting: Acupuncture, Nutrient IVs, and Herbal Supplements.

- Neuropathy and Nerve Pain: IV B Vitamins, Acupuncture, and Low-Dose Naltrexone.

- Weight Loss and Cachexia: Nutritional Support, Amino Acid IV Therapy, and Exercise.

- Digestive Issues and Constipation: Colonics, Herbal Supplements, and Nutrition Guidance.

- Anxiety and Stress: Acupuncture, Mistletoe Therapy, and Low-Dose Naltrexone.

### Scientific Support and References

Research indicates that complementary natural medicine can improve outcomes and reduce recurrence when used alongside conventional treatments. Key studies supporting this approach include:

- Bocci et al., 2006: Study on ozone therapy as an immune modulator and adjunct in cancer therapy. Medical Hypotheses, 67(4), 870-878.

- Hoption Cann et al., 2002: Review of mistletoe therapy as a supportive treatment in cancer patients. Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine, 8(5), 517-527.

- Meyer & Schuster, 2013: Evidence supporting high-dose vitamin C's antioxidant and tumor-fighting properties. Nutrients, 5(11), 5077-5093.

- Takaku et al., 2008: Study on acupuncture's role in relieving chemotherapy-induced nausea. Supportive Care in Cancer, 16(10), 1165-1173.

- Mann et al., 2016: Research indicating that herbal supplements can enhance immune function and alleviate treatment side effects. Journal of Cancer Research and Clinical Oncology, 142(1), 145-156.

- Krebs et al., 2016: Study on the impact of integrative therapies on quality of life in cancer patients. BMC Complementary and Alternative Medicine, 16(1), 243.


For further information on these and additional studies, please contact our office.
