Poly-MVA combines a palladium-lipoic acid complex with B1, B2, B12, and a curated set of trace minerals. The protocol is designed to support several of the body's systems — energy production, the immune response, and quality of life through chronic illness.

This is a specialty infusion frequently used as part of an integrative approach for patients in oncology care or for those navigating long-term immune challenges. As with all of our IV therapies, Poly-MVA is administered after medical clearance and in coordination with your broader care team.
